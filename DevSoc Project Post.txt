Name of Project: WILDCARETECH

Track: Health and Wellness

Tagline of Project: Empowering Wildlife Guardians.

Built With: figma, html, css, dart, jason, nodejs

About the Project: WildCareTech is a groundbreaking application that revolutionizes the way individuals, 
			organizations, and communities actively engage in the protection and preservation of wildlife.
			The users have the ability to report wildlife emergencies, ensuring timely and efficient assistance 
			to animals in need.By harnessing the power of advanced technologies, we can enhance our understanding 
			of wildlife health, improve conservation strategies, and ensure the well-being of our precious wildlife 
			populations.
